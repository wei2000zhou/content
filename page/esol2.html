英语2级

英语2级的主要课程内容包括：

UNIT 1: Culture: this unit is about listening for information about people's lives, listening to tone of voice for feeling, giving personal information and descriptions of people and things and taking part in everyday conversations.
Reading and Writing - Obtaining information from different texts, using a simple dictionary, writing an invitation and writing descriptions of people and things.

UNIT 2: Community News: Is about talking about people' lives and things they did in the past, meeting and greeting people, apologising and giving reasons, describing people.
Reading and Writing -reading about things that happened in the past and about people and their lives.

UNIT 3: Getting around: Listening to travel information, asking and giving travel information, asking for things and responding, asking for and giving directions and talking about events in the past.
Reading and Writing - finding information in timetables, plans and booking forms. Reading about travel problems, filling in booking forms and writing directions.

UNIT 4: Education: Listening for main points in explanations. Understanding discussions about education. Asking for information on the telephone. Asking for and giving personal and factual information and telling people your options.
Reading and Writing - understanding illustrations and plans, reading texts about courses and education. Writing sentences about yourself and your education and using a dictionary correctly.

UNIT 5: Shopping: listening for details about products, following main points in conversation in shops. Asking about and describing products, complaining about things you buy on the telephone and giving instruction for using a washing machine.
Reading and Writing -finding information in advertisements. Reading and writing instructions, filling in forms, writing letters of complain and reading and writing about things to buy.

UNIT 6:Free time and leisure: talking about things you like doing in your free time. Giving, accepting and refusing invitations, asking for and getting information about leisure activities.
Reading and Writing -how to find information about leisure activities. Reading and writing informal invitations and practising new words for leisure activities.

UNIT 7: Changes: Asking and giving information about people's jobs now and before. Talk about hopes for the future. Compare places, people's lives and jobs. Ask questions and show you're listening. Check and clarify information.
Reading and Writing -reading and writing about changes in a community and peoples' lives. Writing about important events in your life.

UNIT 8: Looking for work: listening for general meeting in conversation for work. Listening for details in explanations about job vacancies. Listening for main points to get key information about jobs. Asking for and giving information about job vacancies. Asking for and giving information about jobs.
Reading and Writing -To find information in a job advertisement. Understanding main points in an article on body language. Reading forms for job applications. Writing additional information on an application form. Filling in forms for job applications and work experience
