英语1级

英语1级的主要课程内容包括：

Unit 1  Getting started: Greet others and inroduce yourself, Give information about yourself, Ask others about themselves, Ask for somethng you need, Read a simple text, Spell your name and address, Fill in a simple form, Write sentences about yourself and Understand words, signs and symbols.

Unit 2  Daily life: Say what you do in every day life, Say what you like and don't like, Ask for information, Listen for information, Read about other people and about yourself.

Unit 3  Shopping 1: Ask for information, ask for directions, Give information in shops, Listen for information, Read prices, Read leaflets, Write a shopping list and Read and write notes.

Unit 4  Local transport: Ask for and give travel information, Listen to travel information, Buy a ticket, Read a timetable, Read signs in the station, Read about travel arrangements and Write about travel arrangements.

Unit 5  Health: Give personal information about yourself, Understand questions and explain when your are ill, Make an appointment with the doctor and dentist, Ask for medicines at the chemist's, Read about a doctor's surgery, Know words for the parts of the body, Write appointments in a diary and Read instructions on medicines.

Unit 6  The neighbourhood: Talk about your neighbourhood, Ask where places are, Give simple directions, Understand a recorded message, Ask for opening times, Read about a neighbourhood, Write about your neighbourhood and read simple mapsand leaflets.

Unit 7  Homes: Describe where you live, ask for and give information about homes, talk about your possessions, Make a phone call about house hold problems, read about homes, Get information from advertisements and directories, Fill a simple form, Write about homes and put words in alphabetical order.

Unit 8  Friends and family: Greet and invite people, chat and say goodbye, Talk about other people, family and friends, Order food, Read and write about people, Read a letter from a child's school, Fill in atear-off slip and Read and write addresses.

Unit 9  Shopping 2: Buy clothes and other items, Talk about colours and sizes, Give personal information, Talk about things you like and don't like, Get information leaflets, catalogues, labels, Fill in simple forms and Shopping in a department store.

Unit 10  Going to work: Understand instructions, Working with people, Ask permission, Leave a message, Understands signs and symbols, Read information and Write a note.
