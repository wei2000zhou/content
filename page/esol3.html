英语3级

英语3级的主要课程内容包括：

Unit 1 Our Lives:this unit is about using a variety of strategies for dealing with factual information in texts, to use the language of instructional text to write sentences about different countries. It introduces the world map getting learners to identify and share the discussion and description of their own country. Review geographical concepts, names and location of continents. It provides an opportunity to move outside the classroom using different aspects of grammar, vocabulary and pronunciation.

Unit 2 Sport and exercise: introduces the theme of sport and exercise providing different activities for the students to practice. Reading and identifying verb tenses, adjectives and intensifiers. Speaking and telling stories and saying what you think.
A passport to leisure and fitness teaching learners new words with sports, how to join a leisure centre, asking direct and indirect questions and telephoning for information. Learners practice reading and writing a letter of complaint.

Unit 3 The health service:talks about health care and the National Health Service (NHS). It teaches learners to ask and book appointments or changing arrangements using stress to show urgency. Listening and writing; filling in forms and practising punctuation.
In this unit learners practise reading and writing letters asking for and giving advice. It provides reading about entitlements, going into hospital and practising asking about and explaining future arrangements

Unit 4 Getting to know the UK:this unit is about finding places in Britain, and getting to know their location on the map. Planning where to go, asking and answering questions. Speaking, listening and asking for information; choosing a holiday and booking places to stay. Drafting and wring letters of complaint. Writing descriptions about places using special vocabulary (adjectives).

Unit 5 Technology:this unit is about attitudes to technology; listening and talking about improvement in technology. Listening and discussing a great invention (Braille). Learning new vocabulary, and new verbs for the technology. Listening to instructions; searching the internet and selecting a website. Language; using phrasal verbs, comparisons and learning about imperative (Zero conditional for instruction). Practising surveys and reports.

Unit 6 Life long learning:this unit describes what lifelong learning is, reading and listening about different ways of learning. Reading, and speaking about education, and courses, taking and writing about experiences. Learners practise writing a support statement. Preparing learners for an interview, using interview expressions. Language using, who, which, where and when. Spelling rules adding suffixes.

Unit 7 Local communities:this unit is about meeting the neighbours, speaking and being polite. Finding a nursery and other places using the yellow pages or community notice board. Extension; vocabulary development bringing local maps and using an A-Z. Listening and speaking showing feeling. Planning and writing a letter to a friend. Speaking and listening; giving opinions and agreeing about a public meeting.

Unit 8 Working lives:it introduces local industries and famous cities. Discussing jobs, and opportunities in Liverpool and in your area. A job you like? Listening and talking about jobs (using like and dislikes). Talking about your skills, and qualities choosing the right person for the job. Writing application letters, practising an interview asking questions using 'do's and don'ts' read a job advertisement and find a job
